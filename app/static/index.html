Radius resizes fixed-ratio, circle, and hex selections from the current center. Free mode keeps manual dragging.
Large map mode chunks OSM data for bigger selections. Auto terrain relief controls whether the base is flat or height-varying. Uploaded DEM files override automatic terrain.
Flat base: terrain relief controls are ignored unless Auto terrain relief is on or a DEM is uploaded.
Airport includes runways, taxiways, and aprons as a low layer with parking-lot thickness.
Cut out water removes lakes and ocean from the base so you can place colored material underneath.
Balanced: keeps normal streets plus pedestrian/footway.
Leave small footpaths/service roads off for a cleaner printable street grid. Rail and subway lines are optional and off by default.
